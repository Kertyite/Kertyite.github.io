Hi! I'm a Master's student in Industrial and Management Engineering at Hanyang University ERICA, advised by Prof. Stephane Barde at the [Industrial Operations & Data Analytics (IODA) Lab](https://sites.google.com/view/iodalab).

My research focuses on **Prognostics and Health Management (PHM)** for lithium-ion batteries, with emphasis on deploying deep learning models on resource-constrained edge devices through model compression techniques.

## Research Interests

- Prognostics and Health Management (PHM)
- Time-Series Forecasting
- Edge Computing & Model Compression (Knowledge Distillation, Quantization, Pruning)
- Physics-Informed Neural Networks
